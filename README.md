# HydroSense: IoT Enabled Automated Hydroponic System
Hydroponics is a soil-less farming technique that enables faster plant growth, higher yield and efficient water usage. However, maintaining optimal nutrient concentration, pH balance, temperature, and environmental conditions require continuous manual monitoring and expert knowledge. Small-scale farmers and hobby growers often face crop loss due to improper nutrient dosing, poor oxygenation, and inconsistent environmental control. To overcome these limitations, an automated and intelligent monitoring system is required that can maintain ideal growing conditions with minimal human intervention.
